§ 01
Manifest

Who we are, and what we build.

SimUoPLabs is a student research team within the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of the Peloponnese. Under the guidance of Dr. Georgios Giannopoulos and Dr. Alexandros Kalarakis, we develop high-technology engineering solutions through analysis, experimentation, and manufacturing.

Our work threads computational tools, hands-on prototyping, and student-led research into a single workflow — from CFD and FEM, through SLS printing, to wind-tunnel validation and rocket test fires.

⌬ DSA RACING × SimUoPLabs TECHNICAL SPONSOR

A high-school motorsport team — built on simulation.

SimUoPLabs is a proud sponsor of DSA Racing, a high-school team from the German School of Athens competing in the global F1 in Schools / STEM Racing challenge.

By supporting DSA Racing, we extend our expertise in aerodynamics and 3D manufacturing to student motorsport. Innovation has no age limit — our sponsorship provides the technical resources and mentorship required for success on the track.
