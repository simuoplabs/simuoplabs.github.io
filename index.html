Who We Are

SimUoPLabs bridges academic knowledge with hands-on engineering, simulation, prototyping, manufacturing, and STEM outreach.

SimUoPLabs is a student research team of the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of the Peloponnese. Under the guidance of Dr. Georgios Giannopoulos and Dr. Alexandros Kalarakis, we develop high-technology engineering solutions through analysis, experimentation, and manufacturing.

Our work combines computational tools, practical prototyping, and student-led research. We focus on fluid analysis, structural analysis, electric motors, additive manufacturing, aerospace systems, and engineering education.

Competition pathways: Formula Student / FSAE, CAN-SAT, Czech Rocket Challenge and EuRoC.

STEM actions and certifications, including Certified Onshape Associate preparation.

Fueling the Next Generation

SimUoPLabs supports student innovation beyond the university through technical mentorship and applied engineering collaboration.

DSA Racing × SimUoPLabs

SimUoPLabs is a proud sponsor of DSA Racing, a high-school team from the German School of Athens competing in the global F1 in Schools / STEM Racing challenge.

By supporting DSA Racing, we extend our expertise in aerodynamics and 3D manufacturing to student motorsport. We believe that innovation has no age limit, and our sponsorship helps provide the technical resources and mentorship required for success on the track.
